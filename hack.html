Hi! We're from Pasir Ris East Youth Executive Committee. We conduct events and activities for all of the youths in the Pasir Ris East Community.

We support a variety of interest groups - NERF Games, Board Games, Writing and Vangard Card Fight! We enjoy meeting up and interacting on a regular basis

There is no obligation whatsoever! You do not need to have any prior experience in organising events and activities. We just enjoy meeting awesome people and hope you have a great time.

FAQ

Where are our meetings held?

Meetings are held monthly at one of the meeting rooms in Pasir Ris East Community Centre.

What's the motivation? Are we paid to do this?

No one pays us. We like meeting and organising events for cool youths like you.

How can I get involved?

By attending events hosted by us and volunteering to help out.

How do I propose and plan an event?

You can plan and propose an event during one of our monthly meetings.

What type of events and activities can we propose?

As long as it involves the community and it's healthy.

What is the eligibility for membership

16~35 years old

How long is a term?

The committee will change every two years after a bi-annual general meeting (BGM).

Are there any fees for joining?

No. There are no fees for joining.

What is the application process like?

If we have position available or we have any upcoming event, we'll notify you through phones or emails.

What kind of skills do I need to join?

There is no red tape to who can join us. For as long as you are active, energetic and want to contribute to the community - we want you.

Seriously? This seems to good to be true.

We like being around really interesting and motivated people, and if it's a good fit, we're confident things will just work out. We don't like obligating anyone to do anything.
